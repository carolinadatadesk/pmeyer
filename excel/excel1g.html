Online Tips and Tutorials
 
    The following links will take you to a variety of online learning resources regarding Excel.
  •  From UC Davis - A tutorial on simple spreadsheet calculations from The University of California at Davis.  Also includes tutorial on graphing.
  • Excel Tutorial - Modules on simple workbooks, formulas and functions, formatting, linking documents and creating charts.
  • FAQs - Frequently asked questions about Excel 7.0 from Microsoft.
  • Excel Online - An online Excel tutorial from Computing Services at Canterbury Christ Church College.  Deals with data entry, charts, sorting and statistical functions among other concepts.
  • Excel and Probability - Online tutorial with downloadable worksheets dealing with probability and statistics applications available through Excel.
  • Excel Basics - A simple but well-illustrated primer on the basics from The University of Hawaii.
